Piedmont Stables

Piedmont Stables is located on Redwood Road just down the hill from Skyline Ranch. Piedmont Stables is a “sister” facility to Skyline Ranch, as both are a part of the East Bay Regional Park system and managed by Judi Martin.

Piedmont boarding prices are the same as for Skyline Ranch. Contact us for more information about Piedmont Stables.
