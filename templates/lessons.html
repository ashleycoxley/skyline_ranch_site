Riding Lessons and Training

Skyline Ranch offers riding lessons and training for all ages and levels of experience. Whether you're just beginning riding, starting over after many years, or have competitive goals, our highly qualified trainers can help you reach your goals. We teach balanced seat English lessons, typically leading to either jumping, dressage, or both. For those without a horse, we have many safe and well-educated school horses.

Beginners

Beginning riders take private lessons until they are safe, confident, and capable with horses. First lessons teach grooming and tacking in addition to riding. Once a rider has good control and confidence in the saddle, and can get their lesson horse ready on their own, they are ready to join a semi-private or group lesson.

Advanced Riders

Riders capable of riding confidently in a group are welcome to join advanced group lessons, offered several times a week. Lessons focus on jumping, dressage, and rider fitness. Training

Training is also available for your horse under the care of Judi or one of our other trainers. Our training programs are centered around the ultimate goal of empowering you to achieve your goals. Whether you want to compete or just to feel more confident and effective riding your horse, we can help you get there.

Note: Skyline does not offer guided trail rides or horse rentals.
