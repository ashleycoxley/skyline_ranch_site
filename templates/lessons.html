Riding Lessons

Skyline Ranch offers riding lessons for all ages and all levels of experience. Our safe and well-educated horses have lots to teach our students. Whether you're just beginning riding, starting over after many years, or have competitive goals, our highly qualified instructors can help you reach your goals. We teach balance seat English lessons, typically leading to either jumping, dressage, or both.

Beginners

Beginning riders take private lessons until they are safe, confident, and capable with horses. First lessons teach grooming and tacking in addition to riding. Once a rider has good control and confidence in the saddle, and can get their lesson horse ready on their own, they are ready to join a semi-private or group lesson.

Advanced Riders

Riders capable of riding confidently in a group are welcome to join advanced group lessons, offered several times a week. Lessons focus on jumping, dressage, and rider fitness.
