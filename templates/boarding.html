Horse Boarding

Under Judi Martin's care, Skyline Ranch has offered quality boarding in a friendly community for 30 years. Facilities include a large all-weather riding arena, indoor lunge arena, individual turnout paddocks, and miles of nearby trails in Redwood Regional Park. Most stalls are 10' x 12' and offered at $525 per month.

Included in boarding:
  • Alfalfa and grass hay fed three times daily
  • Daily stall cleaning
  • All-day turnout in individual paddocks, weather permitting
  • For an additional fee: blanketing service

Contact us for availability and more information.
