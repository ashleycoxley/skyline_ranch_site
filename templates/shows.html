Horse Shows and Clinics

At Skyline Ranch, we participate in a full schedule of activities, both at home and on the road. We encourage interested riders to try a variety of activities in order to build their skills and experience, and have fun doing it!

Horse Shows

We compete at horse shows all around Northern California in hunter/jumpers, eventing, and dressage - from the local to the recognized level. Our schedule changes depending on interest, but you can usually find us at the following venues:

  • MHA Shows (Sequoia Arena)
  • Woodside Horse Park
  • Rancho Murieta Equestrian Center
  • Fresno County Horse Park

Clinics

We host monthly dressage clinics with Tracey Lert at Skyline. Serious dressage rider or not - everyone benefits from Tracey's keen eye and depth of knowledge and experience.

We also host a regular schedule of rotating jumping clinicians, travel to local clinics, and go cross country schooling at nearby courses.
